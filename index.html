Logistics

Operational details

Do you cover the capital expenditure?
We offer a co-investment model as standard. Fully-funded installations where we bear total CAPEX are strictly reserved for commercial locations that pass our traffic volume audit. We verify your footfall before approving fully-funded terms.
What are the power requirements?
Standard fast AC chargers require dedicated 3-phase power. Our engineering team conducts a load study during the site audit to determine if your current transformer capacity can handle the specific hardware.
Who handles maintenance?
We operate the network. If a unit faults, our remote diagnostics flag it. We dispatch technicians to resolve hardware issues within forty eight hours. Your staff is not involved.
What is the contract duration?
Standard agreements run for three years to amortize setup costs. Break clauses are defined clearly in the term sheet based on the investment model chosen.

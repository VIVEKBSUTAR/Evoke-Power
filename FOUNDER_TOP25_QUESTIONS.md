# Evoke Power - Founder Intake (Top 25)

Use this short version to collect only launch-critical decisions from the founder/client.

## Business and Offer (5)
1. What is the exact legal company name to use on legal pages?
2. What is the final one-line value proposition for the homepage?
3. Who is the primary target customer (decision-maker persona)?
4. What is the primary conversion goal (form lead, call, WhatsApp)?
5. What are the top 3 differentiators vs competitors?

## Messaging and Content (4)
6. What final hero headline and CTA should we use?
7. Which claims are approved to publish publicly?
8. Which claims or words should never be used?
9. Which 3 FAQs are most important for reducing sales objections?

## Lead Form and Sales Ops (5)
10. Which lead form fields are mandatory?
11. Who receives lead notifications (names and emails)?
12. What is the first-response SLA after form submission?
13. Which CRM is final (HubSpot/Zoho/Salesforce/other)?
14. Do you want auto-reply email after form submission (yes/no)?

## Domain, Hosting, and Access (4)
15. What is the final production domain?
16. Canonical preference: www or non-www?
17. Who owns DNS/domain access and can approve records immediately?
18. Who is the long-term owner admin for Netlify and GitHub?

## Analytics and Marketing (4)
19. What is the GA4 Measurement ID?
20. Are paid ads active now (Google/Meta/LinkedIn)?
21. Share all required pixel IDs for active ad platforms.
22. What are the top 3 success KPIs (for weekly reporting)?

## Legal and Compliance (3)
23. What are the final legal details: registered address, GSTIN, CIN, jurisdiction city?
24. Is cookie consent required before analytics/ad scripts load (yes/no)?
25. Who gives final go-live approval (single owner) and launch date?

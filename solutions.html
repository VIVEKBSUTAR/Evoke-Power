Solutions

Flexible EV charging models for every business stage

Choose a model based on your budget, ownership goals, and operational preference.

01

Buy Charger (Direct Purchase)

Best for businesses and individuals who want full ownership and full revenue control.

  • You buy the charger and own the asset
  • You can set your charging price
  • You keep charging revenue
02

Co-Ownership

Shared investment with EVOKE POWER LLP to reduce upfront cost while enabling revenue generation.

  • Investment is shared
  • Revenue is shared as per agreement
  • Lower upfront burden for partner
03

Franchise / Company Operated

Provide location space while EVOKE POWER LLP installs and operates the station for passive income.

  • Company handles operations
  • Mostly unmanned station workflow
  • Revenue sharing from transactions

Need model guidance for your location?

Our team will help you choose the best structure based on traffic, power, and investment goals.
